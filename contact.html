Frequently Asked Questions

Before contacting us, you might find answers to common questions here:

Q: Is the game really free?

A: Yes! DownDownDown is 100% free to play with no hidden costs, in-app purchases, or premium features.

Q: Why isn't the game loading?

A: Try clearing your browser cache, disabling browser extensions, or using a different browser. Ensure JavaScript is enabled.

Q: Can I play on mobile?

A: Absolutely! The game works on all devices including smartphones and tablets with touch controls.

Q: How do I save my high score?

A: High scores are automatically saved in your browser's local storage. Don't clear your browser data if you want to keep them!

Q: Do you collect my personal data?

A: No! We don't require registration and don't collect personal information. Check our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Can I embed the game on my website?

A: Please contact us at support@downdowndown.live for embedding permissions.
